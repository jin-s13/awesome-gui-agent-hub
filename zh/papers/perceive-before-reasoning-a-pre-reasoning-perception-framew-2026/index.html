arXiv 2026 36.7 method, system

TLDR

提出两阶段框架PRPF，包含轻量级感知器用于干预门控和推理器，减少误触发并提升效率。

评分理由

The paper introduces a novel two-stage design that separates intervention gating from reasoning, addressing goal misalignment and redundant inference. Strengths include clear problem identification and empirical gains on a benchmark. Weaknesses are the narrow focus on proactive mobile agents and lack of generalizability evidence beyond the specific benchmark.

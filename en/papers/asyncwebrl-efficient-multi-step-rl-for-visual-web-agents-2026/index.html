Deep Analysis

Innovations

  • Asynchronous training design with everlasting rollout pool and lightweight screenshot handling, overlapping rollout, gradient update, and policy refresh for up to 2.9x throughput speedup.
  • Replacing per-trajectory normalizer 1/|τ_i| with constant 1/k in multi-step GRPO to decouple trajectory length from gradient weighting, reducing verbose trajectories while maintaining success.

Methodology

AsyncWebRL combines an asynchronous RL system that overlaps rollout, gradient update, and policy refresh using an everlasting rollout pool and lightweight screenshot handling, with an algorithmic change that replaces the per-trajectory normalizer in multi-step GRPO with a constant to prevent down-weighting of failed trajectories. The method is evaluated on the WebGym out-of-distribution test split.

Key Results

AsyncWebRL achieves a new state-of-the-art on WebGym OOD test split with a +5.8% relative improvement over the prior 42.9%, including +42% relative on Medium and +48% relative on Hard slices, while delivering up to 2.9x training throughput speedup.

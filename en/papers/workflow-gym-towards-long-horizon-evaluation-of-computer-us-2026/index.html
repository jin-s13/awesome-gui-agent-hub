Deep Analysis

Innovations

  • Introduction of Workflow-GYM, a benchmark specifically designed for long-horizon GUI tasks in professional domains and specialized software environments.
  • Identification and analysis of key failure modes in current GUI agents, including workflow stage omission, error propagation, objective drift, and insufficient understanding of professional software.

Methodology

The authors constructed Workflow-GYM, a benchmark comprising long-horizon, end-to-end professional workflows that require operating graphical user interfaces of domain-specific software. They then evaluated state-of-the-art AI models on this benchmark to measure success rates and analyze failure patterns.

Key Results

Even the strongest models achieve only slightly above 30% success rate on the benchmark, demonstrating that professional long-horizon GUI workflows remain highly challenging.

Limitations

  • Current agents struggle to maintain long-horizon workflow consistency.
  • Agents frequently exhibit workflow stage omission.
  • Error propagation occurs across steps.
  • Agents suffer from objective drift during task execution.
  • Insufficient understanding of professional software environments.
